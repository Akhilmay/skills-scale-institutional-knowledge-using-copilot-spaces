# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to the OctoAcme project management process documentation. This folder contains guides for running projects at OctoAcme to help teams deliver customer value consistently and predictably.

## Overview of Project Management Processes

OctoAcme follows a structured, lifecycle-driven approach that emphasizes customer value, iterative delivery, and clear ownership. Projects move through Initiation, Planning, Execution, Release, and Retrospective phases—each with defined goals, activities, and artifacts. Roles are clearly defined: Project Managers coordinate delivery and risks, Product Managers drive priorities and measure outcomes, and Developers design and implement features with a focus on quality and testability.

Communication is structured for alignment and rapid risk escalation through daily standups, regular syncs, and stakeholder updates. Quality assurance is embedded throughout: all work is peer-reviewed, tested in continuous integration, and measured against documented acceptance criteria. Lightweight documentation, standardized workflows, and regular retrospectives help OctoAcme scale institutional knowledge and reduce dependency risk.
